Reputation Manager

Summary

The Reputation Manager is a free Google reviews tool for local service businesses. It compares the business against the top 10 local competitors, analyzes review volume, recency, sentiment, keywords, and owner-response quality, then turns those signals into practical recommendations for improving trust and conversion.

Try the free Google reviews tool →

Enter a local service business. The tool compares its Google review presence against the top 10 competitors, analyzes sentiment, and recommends specific ways to improve reputation, responses, and customer trust.

Highlights

  • Free tool focused on Google reviews and local reputation — no cost for business owners to run the analysis.
  • Compares review strength against the top 10 local competitors.
  • Uses sentiment analysis to surface what customers praise, complain about, and repeat across reviews.
  • Generates improvement recommendations for review volume, freshness, keywords, owner responses, and trust signals.
  • Supports Google OAuth so AI can help automatically respond to and manage Google reviews.
  • Connects reputation work back to website conversion, local SEO, and sales readiness.
