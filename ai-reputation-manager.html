Summary

The Reputation Manager expands ServiceProMagic beyond website creation into the trust layer that decides whether local buyers call. It reads reputation signals as sales assets — review volume, freshness, sentiment, owner response quality, local proof, and trust gaps — then translates them into practical actions the business owner can actually use.

Try the live tool →

Enter a local service-business website or company context in the iframe. The tool turns public reputation signals into prioritized recommendations for improving trust before spending more on traffic.

Highlights

  • Reviews reputation signals that affect whether local-service buyers feel safe calling.
  • Surfaces gaps in review volume, freshness, sentiment, owner responses, and proof quality.
  • Creates owner-friendly actions for improving trust before more ad spend.
  • Connects reputation work back to website conversion, local SEO, and sales readiness.
