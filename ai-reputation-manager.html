Summary

The Reputation Manager expands ServiceProMagic beyond website creation into the trust layer that decides whether local buyers call. It treats reviews, recency, sentiment, owner responses, and proof points as conversion assets, then translates those signals into a practical reputation plan.

Highlights

  • Reviews reputation signals that affect local-service buyer confidence.
  • Surfaces gaps in review volume, freshness, sentiment, and response quality.
  • Creates owner-friendly actions for improving trust before more ad spend.
  • Connects reputation work back to website conversion, local SEO, and sales readiness.
