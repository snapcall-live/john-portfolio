Summary

ROICalcMagic.com is the full SaaS product and business system: a platform that turns a SaaS company’s value proposition into interactive ROI calculators, executive ROI summaries, and buyer-ready business cases that help sales teams prove value earlier in the deal.

Highlights

  • Acquired first five customers.
  • Turns vague value claims into quantified business impact.
  • Supports enterprise sales teams with calculators, assumptions, and buyer-facing outputs.
  • Summarizes ROI, savings, payback, assumptions, and business-case logic in buyer-friendly language.
  • Built to make value selling faster, more credible, and easier to operationalize.
