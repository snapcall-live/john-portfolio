Form Cannon

A GTM automation console that sources, qualifies, scores, and engages website prospects with personalized demo-site outreach.

Lead sourcingICP filteringDemo generationAutomated outreach

Summary

Form Cannon is a GTM engine for finding service businesses with weak websites, building a better demo site, and attempting to submit a personalized message through their web form. It combines lead sourcing, qualification, website scoring, automated demo generation, form submission, fallback email, and reporting into one operator console.

Highlights

  • Sources leads from Outscraper and dedupes incoming batches.
  • Removes companies that do not match the ICP target audience.
  • Assesses whether a web form can be submitted automatically.
  • Scores website quality to prioritize poor-fit / high-opportunity targets.
  • Builds a demo site before outreach so the message has a concrete proof point.
  • Submits personalized webform messages, with email fallback when forms fail.
  • Includes reporting to manage cost, throughput, queue health, and submission speed.
