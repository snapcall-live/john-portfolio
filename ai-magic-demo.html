Magic Demo

An AI-powered demo system that presents a prospect with what feels like a fully custom ROI calculator build, while delaying the expensive calculator generation until the prospect actually clicks.

ROI demo generation Personalized sales pages Brand QA Cost-efficient GTM

Summary

Magic Demo presents the ROI Calculator as a fully built custom build for each prospect, but the calculator is not actually created until the customer clicks the link. This keeps costs low while still delivering a stunning, highly customized experience.

Live demo preview →

The iframe shows a working Magic Demo sales pitch page that personalizes the experience before revealing the interactive ROI calculator.

Highlights

  • Designed an end-to-end AI generation pipeline from website URL to live calculator.
  • Combined website research, brand extraction, formula generation, QA scoring, and publishing into one workflow.
  • Built Magic Demo sales pages that personalize the pitch before revealing the interactive ROI calculator.
  • Added automated brand QA to compare generated calculators against source websites for color, typography, layout, and visual fidelity.
  • Implemented deployment, smoke testing, and revision verification workflows on Google Cloud Run.
  • Preloads slow, inexpensive, serialized steps at link creation, then saves faster, more expensive, parallelized steps for click time — keeping CAC costs low while delivering extraordinarily customized experiences.
Cost architectureLink creation: 1/10th of a penny · Click-time generation: 1 penny
