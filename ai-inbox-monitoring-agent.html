Inbox Monitoring Agent

An AI email operations agent that monitors 87 inboxes, handles common requests automatically, and escalates uncertain cases into Telegram channels for team review.

Email ops87 inboxesAuto-responseHuman escalation

Summary

The Inbox Monitoring Agent turns a large, fragmented inbox footprint into a managed operating system. It watches 87 inboxes, identifies routine requests it can answer safely, and routes ambiguous or sensitive messages to Telegram channels where the team can monitor, review, and respond.

Highlights

  • Monitors 87 inboxes across the business footprint.
  • Automatically responds to common, repeatable requests.
  • Escalates uncertain, sensitive, or high-judgment messages instead of guessing.
  • Uses Telegram channels as the human review and response layer.
  • Creates a practical human-in-the-loop system for scaling email operations without losing oversight.
