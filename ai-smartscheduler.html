AI invention 09

SmartScheduler

A safe, interactive booking experience that shows how a service business can turn website interest into scheduled jobs without making the customer call or wait for a callback.

Book onlineSMS opt-inLead conversionService operations
Open live example ↗

Summary

SmartScheduler turns scheduling into a conversion layer for home service websites. Instead of a passive contact form, it gives customers a guided way to request service, choose timing, provide job details, and opt into SMS updates.

Highlights

  • Interactive “Book Online” flow shown in safe demo mode.
  • Captures service type, urgency, contact info, preferred time, address, and job notes.
  • Includes SMS consent language without triggering actual text confirmations.
  • Demonstrates how AI-powered websites can connect lead capture, scheduling, and customer communication.
  • Designed to reduce friction between “I need help” and “the job is booked.”

Demo safety

This form is intentionally wired as a front-end demo only. Submitting it shows a sample confirmation inside the page — it does not create a real appointment or send SMS messages.
