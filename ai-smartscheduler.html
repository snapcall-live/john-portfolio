SmartScheduler

A guided “Book Online” intake and scheduling flow that captures job details, preferred timing, contact information, and clear SMS opt-in language while keeping the portfolio demo safely sandboxed.

Summary

SmartScheduler turns scheduling into a conversion layer for home service websites. Instead of a passive contact form, it walks customers through the intake details a dispatcher needs, then shows how service requests can move toward scheduling and communication without extra phone tag.

Highlights

  • Interactive “Book Online” flow shown in safe demo mode.
  • Captures service type, urgency, contact info, preferred time, address, and job notes.
  • Uses clear SMS consent wording for appointment updates and follow-up while this portfolio iframe blocks real submission.
  • Demonstrates how AI-powered websites can connect lead capture, scheduling, and customer communication.
  • Designed to reduce friction between “I need help” and “the job is booked.”

Demo safety

The real form is shown in a sandboxed iframe so visitors can see the actual experience without triggering appointment creation or SMS confirmations from this portfolio page.
