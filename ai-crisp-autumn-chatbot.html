Summary

The Crisp Autumn Chatbot is different from a generic embedded website bot. Autumn handles visitor responses with context from the actual work behind ServiceProMagic: what has been built, why it was built, how the supporting tools work, and what decisions shaped the site. That gives the chat experience a stronger operational foundation than a scripted FAQ widget.

Highlights

  • Runs through Crisp on the live ServiceProMagic.com site.
  • Uses Autumn, an OpenClaw-powered bot, to respond with project-specific context.
  • Understands the site, supporting tools, build decisions, and service positioning.
  • Can explain capabilities, answer questions, and guide prospects with deeper continuity than a normal chatbot.
  • Creates a live example of AI as an operating teammate, not just a chat bubble.
