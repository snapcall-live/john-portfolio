Value Proposition Extractor

An AI analysis layer that studies a SaaS company’s messaging and extracts the measurable outcomes that should power a credible ROI story.

Value propMessaging analysisOutcome mappingBuyer pain

Summary

The Value Proposition Extractor identifies where a product creates measurable business value — saved time, increased revenue, reduced cost, lower churn, productivity gains, risk reduction, or faster execution.

Highlights

  • Analyzes SaaS websites and product positioning.
  • Converts marketing claims into measurable business outcomes.
  • Separates vague benefits from ROI-worthy value drivers.
  • Creates the foundation for stronger calculators and outreach messaging.
