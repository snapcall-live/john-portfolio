SnapCall Tablet Application

A restaurant tablet application for receiving AI-captured orders and sending them to the kitchen, based on the SnapCall.live operating concept.

Tablet appKitchen handoffRestaurant opsOrder routing

Summary

The Tablet Application is the operational surface for SnapCall: a restaurant-facing app where AI-captured orders can be reviewed, accepted, and sent to the kitchen.

Highlights

  • Displays incoming customer orders for restaurant staff.
  • Supports kitchen handoff instead of leaving orders trapped in a call transcript.
  • Creates a practical bridge between AI voice ordering and restaurant operations.
  • Designed around the SnapCall.live concept for sending orders to the kitchen.
