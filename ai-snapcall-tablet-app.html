Summary

This is a retained placeholder for a future SnapCall showcase. The tablet application is the planned operational surface for SnapCall: a restaurant-facing app where AI-captured orders can be reviewed, accepted, and sent to the kitchen. It is not yet completed or showcase-ready.

Highlights

  • Displays incoming customer orders for restaurant staff.
  • Supports kitchen handoff instead of leaving orders trapped in a call transcript.
  • Creates a practical bridge between AI voice ordering and restaurant operations.
  • Designed around the SnapCall.live concept for sending orders to the kitchen.
