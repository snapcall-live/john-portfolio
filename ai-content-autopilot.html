Content Autopilot

An AI-powered content system for home service businesses that can turn market knowledge, services, FAQs, seasonal demand, and local customer questions into useful website and marketing content.

Content opsLocal SEOCustomer educationMarketing automation
Open blog example ↗

Summary

Content Autopilot is an AI-assisted content queue for service businesses. It creates a multi-week publishing plan, prepares article topics, and helps operators consistently publish useful content without managing a full editorial calendar by hand.

Highlights

  • Manages a 10-week content queue for a service-business customer.
  • Shows upcoming and published posts in one workflow.
  • Includes “Ready to Publish” status and regenerate controls.
  • Flags similar topics so the queue can be improved before publishing.
  • Supports the promise: “Set it and forget it” content operations for local businesses.
