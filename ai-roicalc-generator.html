Summary

The ROI Calculator Generation Process is the engine behind ROICalcMagic. It converts messy product messaging, target-buyer pain, and value claims into calculator structure: inputs, formulas, assumptions, outputs, executive summary copy, and the buyer-facing story.

Demo link workflow →

Generated calculator concepts are meant to become shareable demos, giving prospects a concrete business-case asset instead of a wait-for-us-to-build-it promise.

Highlights

  • Builds a calculator from company, product, ICP, and value-prop context.
  • Creates financial assumptions and editable input ranges.
  • Generates buyer-facing outputs such as ROI, savings, payback period, annual value, and an executive-ready summary.
  • Compresses a traditionally custom consulting/build process into a repeatable AI workflow.
  • Feeds the demo link and delivery workflow so prospects can see a working calculator concept quickly instead of waiting on a custom build cycle.
