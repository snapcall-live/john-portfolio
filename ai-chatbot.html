Summary

The AI Chatbot is not just a chatbot for ServiceProMagic.com — it is a product capability ServiceProMagic can enable for its customers. It turns a home-service website into an interactive front door that answers questions, collects context, and converts anonymous visitors into qualified service opportunities.

Highlights

  • Answers common customer questions directly on the service-business website.
  • Qualifies the job by asking about service type, urgency, location, and issue details.
  • Captures contact information so the business can follow up quickly.
  • Guides visitors toward booking requests instead of leaving them on a passive page.
  • Shows how ServiceProMagic connects answers, lead capture, and conversion in one customer journey.
