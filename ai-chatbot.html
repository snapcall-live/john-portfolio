AI Chatbot

A standard AI intake and booking assistant for every ServiceProMagic.com customer: trained from the business website and online presence, able to answer questions, gather job details, recommend next steps, and connect with SmartScheduler for live appointment booking.

Summary

Our AI Chatbot comes standard for every ServiceProMagic.com customer. It trains itself from the customer’s current website information and other signals available across their online presence, then turns that knowledge into an interactive front door that answers questions, gathers job context, recommends next steps, captures contact details, and can integrate with SmartScheduler so customers can book and confirm appointments live through AI chat.

Highlights

  • Answers common customer questions using information from the business website and online presence.
  • Includes AI-powered “train your chatbot” functionality inside the customer dashboard and onboarding process.
  • Qualifies the job by asking about service type, urgency, location, and issue details.
  • Captures contact information so the business can follow up quickly.
  • Guides visitors toward clear next steps instead of leaving them on a passive page.
  • Integrates with SmartScheduler so customers can book and confirm appointments live through AI chat.
  • Comes standard for every ServiceProMagic.com customer.
