Marketing Strategy

A free AI strategy tool that can infer the service, local market, buyer, and competitive angle from a URL or short business description, then turn that into clearer positioning and owner-friendly marketing direction.

GTM strategy Market positioning Messaging Owner-friendly planning
Open strategy tool ↗

Summary

The Marketing Strategy tool is the planning layer before website changes or campaigns. When a URL provides enough context, it infers what the business sells, who it serves, where it can win locally, which offers deserve priority, and which messages should guide the website, content, and follow-up motion. When the site is thin or missing, the same workflow can fall back to a short business description.

Highlights

  • Built as a free tool / lead magnet inside the ServiceProMagic ecosystem.
  • Designed to show immediate value before a sales conversation.
  • Clarifies target customer, offer focus, local differentiation, likely buyer objections, and campaign priorities when the available source context supports it.
  • Turns AI output into a practical business workflow, not just generic content.
  • Supports the larger ServiceProMagic positioning: strategy before templates.
