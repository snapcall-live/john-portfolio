Marketing Strategy

A free AI strategy tool that infers the service, market, customer, and competitive angle from business context, then turns that into clearer positioning and owner-friendly marketing direction.

Summary

The Marketing Strategy tool is the planning layer before website changes or campaigns. From a business URL or short company description, it infers what the business sells, who it serves, where it can win locally, which offers deserve priority, and which messages should guide the website, content, and follow-up motion.

Highlights

  • Built as a free tool / lead magnet inside the ServiceProMagic ecosystem.
  • Designed to show immediate value before a sales conversation.
  • Clarifies target customer, offer focus, local differentiation, likely buyer objections, and campaign priorities.
  • Turns AI output into a practical business workflow, not just generic content.
  • Supports the larger ServiceProMagic positioning: strategy before templates.
