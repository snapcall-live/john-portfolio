Summary

The Improvement Hub turns a website analysis into something tangible: a live, navigable example of what a stronger version of the business website could look like. It helps prospects understand the gap between their current site and a more strategic, conversion-focused web presence.

Highlights

  • Shows a generated improvement concept instead of a static report.
  • Connects analysis, positioning, copy, and design into a live experience.
  • Useful as both a customer-facing demo and a sales enablement asset.
  • Demonstrates how AI can compress consultative website strategy into an interactive proof point.
