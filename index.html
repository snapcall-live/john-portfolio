About me

I build growth engines — but what I really build is belief.

My work sits at the intersection of revenue, partnerships, leadership, and practical AI. But underneath the titles and metrics is a simple operating belief: people do their best work when they understand the mission, trust the system, and feel part of something worth building.

I have spent my career turning messy go-to-market problems into momentum — building teams, partner ecosystems, sales motions, enablement systems, and now AI-powered tools that help companies move faster. I care about the number, but I care just as much about how the number gets built: with clarity, accountability, creativity, and respect for the people doing the work.

The through-line is builder energy. I like taking something underdeveloped, overlooked, or chaotic and giving it structure, story, and force. Whether I’m leading a global partner team, coaching sellers, building an AI venture, or helping partners see a bigger opportunity, my best work happens when strategy becomes something people can actually use.
