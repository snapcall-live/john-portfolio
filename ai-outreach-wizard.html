Outreach Wizard

An outbound sales asset workflow for finding SaaS prospects, shaping calculator offers, and generating personalized messages around measurable buyer value instead of generic feature pitches.

OutreachGTMPersonalizationPipeline

Summary

The Outreach Wizard is the outbound layer for ROICalcMagic. It helps identify target SaaS companies, frame the calculator opportunity, generate useful first-touch copy, and package the calculator as a concrete sales asset rather than an abstract pitch.

Highlights

  • Targets SaaS companies where ROI calculators can support sales.
  • Personalizes outreach around the company’s value proposition and likely buyer pain.
  • Connects the offer to sales friction, value-selling needs, and a specific calculator asset.
  • Feeds follow-up and pipeline tracking into the CRM workflow.
