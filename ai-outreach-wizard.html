Outreach Wizard

An outbound sales asset workflow for finding SaaS prospects, generating calculator angles, and producing personalized first-touch copy around measurable buyer value instead of generic feature pitches.

Outreach GTM Personalization Pipeline

Summary

The Outreach Wizard is the outbound layer for ROICalcMagic. It combines prospect queues, sender limits, lead ownership, strategy instructions, and AI-generated email drafts so a sales team can package a custom ROI calculator as a concrete reason to start a conversation.

Highlights

  • Targets SaaS companies where ROI calculators can support value selling.
  • Creates a prospect-specific calculator angle before writing the outreach.
  • Generates outbound copy tied to buyer pain, measurable value, and a concrete ROI asset.
  • Supports sender usage controls, draft review, queue search, and lead ownership for outbound operations.
