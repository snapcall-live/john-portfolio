ROI Assumption Builder

An AI financial-logic layer that creates defensible calculator assumptions, input ranges, and formulas from a product’s value drivers.

Financial assumptionsROI logicBenchmarksValue model

Summary

The ROI Assumption Builder makes the calculator credible. It structures the assumptions behind the model so the output feels grounded, adjustable, and believable to a buyer.

Highlights

  • Creates editable assumptions instead of hard-coded claims.
  • Maps value drivers to formulas, ranges, and buyer inputs.
  • Supports conservative, moderate, and aggressive scenarios.
  • Gives sales teams a stronger financial story without requiring spreadsheet consulting.
