Summary

ServiceProMagic is a fully built SaaS company for home service businesses, created with AI-assisted market research, competitor analysis, positioning, conversion copy, and lead capture. The site is designed to turn trade, city, and customer context into pages that make the phone ring.

Highlights

  • Built a live SaaS workflow that can generate a polished service-business website in minutes instead of the traditional agency timeline.
  • Developed AI-assisted research, competitor analysis, service-page generation, positioning, conversion copy, CTAs, trust signals, lead capture, and plain-English website editing.
  • Architected and launched the growth engine, including lead identification, website scoring, and automated outreach by email, form submission, and direct mail.
  • Forged a partnership to deploy and scale a Philippines-based outside sales team for ServiceProMagic.
  • Positioned the product beyond website builder territory: local SEO pages, AI-search-ready content, lead generation, reviews/trust presentation, and trade-specific conversion strategy.
