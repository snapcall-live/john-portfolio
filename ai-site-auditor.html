Site Auditor

A QA tool that helps human reviewers verify calculator quality and brand adherence by showing the generated calculator beside the customer’s website.

QA workflowBrand adherenceCalculator reviewHuman-in-the-loop

Summary

Site Auditor helps human users QA generated calculators before delivery. It places the calculator in one pane and the customer website in the other so reviewers can compare brand, tone, terminology, visual fit, and accuracy in context.

Highlights

  • Displays the generated calculator and customer website side by side.
  • Helps reviewers catch brand, messaging, and design mismatches.
  • Supports human-in-the-loop QA before a calculator is shipped.
  • Makes calculator review faster, more visual, and more defensible.
